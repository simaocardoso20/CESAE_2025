The Audi R8 is one of the most iconic cars in the modern automotive world. Developed by Audi Sport GmbH, the R8 is a supercar that blends elegant design, extreme performance, and cutting-edge technology. With a high-revving V10 engine, quattro all-wheel drive, and precision engineering, it delivers an exhilarating driving experience, while its aerodynamic exterior and luxurious interior make it a perfect balance of style and performance.

The Audi RS7 Sportback is one of the most powerful and refined models in the Audi Sport lineup. This luxury sedan with a coupe-like design combines elegance, space, and comfort with supercar-level performance. Its advanced engine, quattro all-wheel-drive, and modern driver-assistance systems deliver an exhilarating drive while remaining practical for everyday use.
